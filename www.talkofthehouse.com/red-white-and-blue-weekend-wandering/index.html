Red, White, and Blue Weekend Wandering

print

source

I have been busy working in the yard around here…with thunderstorms appearing every single evening this week to water my new plants.  I’ll have photos for you soon!  Meanwhile, with the approaching 4th of July week ahead of us, I thought you might like to see some patriotic eye candy (and there are a couple of fun links at the end.) If you want to stock up on all things red, white, and blue, you can do some serious shopping at Lexington Company.  How in the world have I missed knowing about them??  I just discovered them yesterday.  Their products seem very much like Ralph Lauren’s, and the prices are much like the RL line, too.  (Click here to shop.)

lexingtoncompany.com

And if you would like to purchase the estate of a great American icon, I have one for you – complete with a flag on the wall.  Katharine Hepburn’s property is still on the market in Connecticut through Sotheby’s. (You can see the complete home tour by clicking here.)

sothebysrealty.com

There is quite a bit of white with just a little touch of blue in the rooms.  You can add in some red if you would like. 🙂

One last bit of wandering for you this weekend…

Here’s an early 4th of July parade.  Just click here or on the video link below to enjoy it.

marthastewart.com

I hope to be back here on Sunday with some red, white, and blue from around our home.  Until then I wish you a weekend filled with fun, food, friends, and family.

Happy weekend!
