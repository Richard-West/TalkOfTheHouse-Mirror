It took quite a while for me to convince my husband that we could build a new home and have what we wanted for about the same price as buying an older home. When we finally decided to pursue a lot I called the realtor only to discover that the lot had been taken off the market that day as the owner had lost his battle with cancer and the kids were going to take some time to sort things out. A year later I drove by again to see the for sale sign was out again. I made the call again and the rest is history. Our forever home is quite large and our lake house is about half the size at 2400 square feet. We were able to find a great builder who built and finished the main floor of 1200 square feet in 90 days. We spent the next 6 months finishing the basement for the remaining 1200 square feet. This may be the best decision we have ever made. Our adult children love coming here and we live here 6 months each year since it is located within 30 minutes of my husband’s job and our church and within 45 minutes of our forever home. Keep dreaming and planning! I can’t wait to see this dream come to reality!
