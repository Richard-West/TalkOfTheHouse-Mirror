Even the old gas stations and road side hotels from the 20′ and 30’s are all but gone and forgotten. I have been looking for pictures and have taken pictures of as many of these old places that I can, which doesn’t amount to very many. I try and plan on making paintings (pastel is my medium) of these forgotten places and try to show that they were once a homes, business places where families shared joy, lived life, shared hearbreak and lost love ones.
