The 2016 Southern Living Idea House – Celebration Cottage

Laurel, right outside of Birmingham, Alabama – the magazine’s own hometown andÂ they chose a hometown architect/designer, Bill Ingram, to be the brains behind it. Â Before they even began work on their rooms, the group was shown photos from old Southern Living articles and homes to use for inspiration in designing their spaces.
