More Biltmore Estate shopping is also available at a few shops at the entrance gate, and then it gets confusing. At the center of the village is The Cathedral of All Souls, the church that George Vanderbilt had constructed from 1896-1905 for parishioners who lived in the village.

The homes were constructed in the Tudor architectural style. Â There is a good article with the timeline of the construction of Biltmore Village here.
