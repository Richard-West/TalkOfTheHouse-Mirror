Blackberry Farm..More Than the Rooms

print

source

The last time we were here, we were talking about the fabulous accommodations in the farmhouse and cottages at  Blackberry Farm.  Now I don’t want you to get the idea that I want to visit Blackberry Farm just for its rooms.  Oh no…I also want to go to enjoy its food.

stylemepretty.com

gardenandgun.com

It has been ranked at the top of many lists (Zagat’s, Travel and Leisure, Fodor’s..among others) as having the best service, best resort, best inn, and/or best food.  In fact 10 days ago, Andrew Harper’s Hideaway Report named it the #1 Food and Wine Resort and the #2 Hideaway in the country.

stylemepretty.com

Blackberry Farm is famous for its Foothills Cuisine – food from the south, but not necessarily your typical southern cuisine.

stylemepretty.com

They have mastered the farm-to-table movement, and then they have added their own creative spin on things.   Here is a sample of their lunch menu. (The macaroni and cheese sounds like the ultimate comfort food.)

blackberryfarm.com

For me to consider a meal memorable, not only does the food have to taste wonderful, but the setting has to also be beautiful.  Blackberry Farm rises to the top with both of those demands.  Just look at all the places where you can dine during your stay.

There is the Barn.source

cntraveler.com

There is the Yallarhammer pavilion.

blackberryfarm.com

gardenandgun.com

In the boathouse you can have a private dinner, or they can accommodate a group of up to 18 guests.

source

tennesseebridal.com

And then there are the well-used outdoor tables that dot the property.

stylemepretty.com

Which place would you choose for dining?

I would have a hard time choosing just one!

Blackberry Farm also sells many of its food items that they can ship right to your home for you.  You can order directly from the Farm or from Williams-Sonoma.  How about a breakfast collection of all the items below?

blackberryfarm.com

Or would you prefer a southern foods collection?williams-sonoma.com

A year or so ago, one of my children bought me the Blackberry Farm cookbook.  It is more than just a collection of their inventive recipes.  It takes you through all four seasons of gardening at the farm and weaves in the stories of the people in the area.  And the photography in the book is breathtaking – as I am sure you can imagine.

Click the link below the photo for my Amazon Affiliate link:

The Blackberry Farm Cookbook: Four Seasons of Great Food and the Good Life

There is a new book from Sam Beall due to be released at the end of October.

The Foothills Cuisine of Blackberry Farm: Recipes and Wisdom from Our Artisans, Chefs, and Smoky Mountain Ancestors

AND on November 8 from 1:00-3:00 p.m., Sam Beall will be in Atlanta signing his new book and doing a cooking demonstration at the Williams Sonoma in Lenox Square.  I would love to be there for that, but unfortunately that is a workday for me…maybe I can talk my son in Atlanta into going for me. 🙂 So for those of us who cannot attend, here is a video of Sam doing a cooking demonstration for all of us to enjoy.

I hope you see that there is so much more than just the rooms at Blackberry Farm to appreciate, and I haven’t even mentioned all the activities they have available for their guests.  Among other things, there is fishing, hiking, canoeing, horseback riding, tennis, swimming and biking. There are classes you can take in cooking and gardening, and the Farm has a spa.  There is truly something for everyone.

tripadvisor.com

My “something” would be simply to soak up all the beauty of the area and indulge in their divine food.

How about you?

If you had your choice of activities, what would you choose?
