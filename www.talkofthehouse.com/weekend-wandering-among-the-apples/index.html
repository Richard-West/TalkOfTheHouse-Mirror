Weekend Wandering Among the Apples

print

source

This weekend I would love to be wandering around in an apple orchard.  Wouldn’t that be fun? The u-pick orchards in north Georgia have apples ripe and ready for picking.  Plus they have tons of entertaining things for the entire family like animal petting areas and tractor rides.  Ellijay claims to be the “apple capital” of Georgia, and there is even a stretch of highway there that they call “apple alley” because there are so many orchards. The Red Apple Barn is one you might want to check out if you are lucky enough to be in the area.

While we are talking about apples, take a look at this gorgeous table from Style Me Pretty.  I thought it was a wonderful setting!  If you click on the link below, you can see all the photos from the shoot.

stylemepretty.com
